Design Decisions
WHY PROMPT EXPANSION?
Short prompts like "happy" collapse to similar BERT regions. Expansion pushes the vector into a more specific semantic space.
WHY NORMALIZE?
Without L2 norm, 768-dim BERT dominates cosine signal. Mood + context contribute <3% regardless of their values.
WHY HARD FILTER?
Soft weighting can't guarantee mood exclusion. Restricting the candidate pool is the only reliable way to prevent cross-mood results.
WHY FAISS?
numpy cosine at 50K songs: ~100ms. FAISS IndexFlatIP: ~1ms. Scales to 100M songs at ~20ms.
CONTEXT WEIGHT 4×?
Activity tags (Good for…) were underutilized — without heavy weighting, "gym music" and "study music" return identical results.
